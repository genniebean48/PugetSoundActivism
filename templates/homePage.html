Our Mission

This website aims to elevate the voices of Puget Sound activism oriented clubs as well as provide them with a cohesive platform to share their events such as protests, sit ins and club meetings. Through event postings and club profiles, students will have access to event information such as times, places, resources and details in a centralized location.
